# Knosi is adding paid plans

*A note to existing knosi.xyz users.*

## What's changing

Knosi is introducing a paid Pro plan. The **hosted service at knosi.xyz** is adding limits to the free tier and a $9/month Pro upgrade.

**Self-hosting is unaffected.** If you run Knosi yourself via Docker, nothing changes. All features remain open-source and free, forever.

## What happens to my account?

Existing knosi.xyz users get a **30-day Pro grace window** starting the day we ship. During these 30 days you keep full access to every feature, same as before.

After 30 days, accounts transition to the Free tier unless you upgrade. Your **data is never deleted or hidden** — existing notes, portfolio positions, focus history, and captured AI chats all remain visible.

## What's on each plan

### Free
- 20 Ask AI calls per day (bring your own key / Claude daemon / local model)
- 50 notes
- 100 MB image storage
- 3 share links
- Full editor, tags, search, dark mode, all core features

### Pro — $9/month or $90/year
- 80 Ask AI calls per day (Knosi AI included — no setup)
- Unlimited notes
- 10 GB image storage
- Unlimited share links
- Portfolio Tracker, Focus Tracker, OSS Projects, Claude Capture
- Priority email support

## What does "read-only" mean for existing data over the Free limits?

If you go Free with more data than the Free tier allows:

- **Your notes / images / portfolio / focus / capture history stay readable, always.**
- You can't *create new* items above the limit — e.g., if you have 80 notes, you can't create note #81 without upgrading or deleting some.
- Pro modules (Portfolio, Focus, OSS Projects, Claude Capture) switch to **read-only**: you can still view existing data, export it, but can't add / edit / delete from within Knosi.

## I want to leave — can I export my data?

Yes, anytime. Go to **Settings → Export** and download a full JSON backup (every user-owned table) or a Markdown archive (notes only). This is available on every plan, Free or Pro, even after cancellation.

## Upgrading

Click **Upgrade to Pro** from any lock screen, or visit [knosi.xyz/pricing](https://knosi.xyz/pricing).
