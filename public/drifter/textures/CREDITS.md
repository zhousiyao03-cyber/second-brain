## stars-night.png

- Source page: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Night_sky_with_moon_and_stars_over_cotton_field_in_Batesville,_Texas.jpg
- Original title: "Night sky with moon and stars over cotton field in Batesville, Texas"
- Author: U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), via Flickr (https://www.flickr.com/photos/usdagov/50336144757/)
- License: U.S. Government work — Public Domain (no rights reserved)
- Processing: downloaded 3840px JPG, top 960px (sky region) cropped, resized to 2048x512, levels adjusted to enhance stars, luminance used as alpha mask (dark sky -> transparent, stars -> opaque), quantized to PNG-8 colormap with alpha. Result is a wide transparent star field suitable for tiling inside the window mask.
